Our Story

Founded in 2015 by Chef Odo Mensah, Odo's Kitchen brings authentic Ghanaian culinary traditions to Cape Coast. Our ingredients are sourced daily from local markets and farms, ensuring the freshest flavors in every dish.

We take pride in preparing meals the traditional way, using recipes passed down through generations. Our restaurant overlooks the beautiful Cape Coast shoreline, providing a perfect dining atmosphere.
